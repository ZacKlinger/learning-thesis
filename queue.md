## Wishlist additions to make this Saturday

- **Bryk et al. (2010) *Organizing Schools for Improvement: Lessons
  from Chicago* — full book.** The symposium deck delivered the
  headline finding but not the underlying methodology. The full book
  would let the structural-conditions reframing become essay-grade
  evidence. Paywalled; would need an institutional library copy.
- **Huberman, Bitter, Anthony & O'Day (2014) — Report 1 of the AIR
  Deeper Learning study (*The Shape of Deeper Learning*).** Describes
  *what* the network schools did — the strategies, structures, and
  cultures the Zeiser outcomes are attributed to. The mechanism
  companion to Zeiser. ERIC may have it under a related ED ID.
- **Bitter, Taylor, Zeiser & Rickles (2014) — Report 2 of the AIR
  Deeper Learning study (*Providing Opportunities for Deeper
  Learning*).** Describes *how much* opportunity students at network
  schools had to engage in deeper-learning practices. The mediator
  between Report 1 (what schools did) and Report 3 / Zeiser
  (outcomes). Also likely on ERIC.
- **Lamon, Reeve & Caswell (1999) or Hewitt (2002) — CSILE /
  Knowledge Forum follow-up evaluations.** S&B 1994 cites Scardamalia
  et al. 1992 (self-citation) for the headline empirical claim. The
  later evaluation literature would either substantiate or qualify
  the magnitude.
